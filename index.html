Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how the Bulk WebP Converter extension handles your information. We wrote it in simple language so it’s clear and easy to understand.

Product: Bulk WebP Converter Version: 1.0

What data we collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. Bulk WebP Converter processes all images directly in your browser. None of your files, preferences, or activities are ever uploaded to external servers.

Permissions and why we need them

The extension uses only the minimal permissions required for its features:

  • Downloads — required to save converted images or ZIP archives to your computer.
  • Storage — used to remember simple settings such as your preferred quality level or resize options. This data remains only in your browser.
  • ActiveTab — enables the popup interface to stay active while you work, preventing accidental closure.

How the extension works

Bulk WebP Converter allows you to drag and drop images into the extension, convert them to WebP format, and download them individually or as a ZIP file. All processing is handled locally within your browser, ensuring full privacy.

Third-party services

This extension does not use analytics, ads, or tracking services. No third-party servers are involved in its operation.

Cookies and local storage

Bulk WebP Converter does not use cookies. It may use chrome.storage to save your quality preferences or resizing choices. This information is stored only in your browser and never transmitted elsewhere.

Data retention

Since no data is collected externally, nothing is stored outside of your browser. Any preferences you save remain until you uninstall the extension or clear your browser data.

Security

All processing happens within Chrome’s secure extension environment. Bulk WebP Converter does not execute remote code, ensuring your images never leave your device. Updates are delivered only through the official Chrome Web Store.

Your rights

You are in full control. You can uninstall the extension at any time. If you have privacy-related questions or requests, please contact us at the email provided below.

Children

This extension is a general-use tool and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any data from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If changes are made, the updated version will be posted here with a revised effective date.
